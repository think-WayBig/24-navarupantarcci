Charity & Donations

We invite donors from India and abroad to be a part of SGB Family and participate in the noble cause of service to child’s welfare and progress. Each penny that is contributed by you helps to improve and uplift the lives of needy, destitute, abandoned and orphan children. There is provision of 80G tax rebate available for sending donations to mother foundation account. You can send your contribution / Donation personally to our institution's office or through a Cheque / Demand Draft / Net Banking / Western Union / Money gram etc. in favour of " SGB INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION " payable at " Grain market, Mullanpur, Ludhiana " Punjab, INDIA. There are separate bank accounts for contribution in India from India and for sending contributions from out of country.

For any query, you can call us directly or e-mail us.
