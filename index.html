Welcome to FISS

Founded in 2016, the Foundation for Integrated Support and Solutions (FISS) is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering inclusive social growth in Northeast India. Our mission is to empower vulnerable communities through integrated initiatives in education, livelihoods, environment, health, nutrition, sports, culture, and rehabilitation.
At FISS, we believe that all people deserve the chance to reach their full potential. That's why we work tirelessly to break down barriers and provide access to resources, skills, and opportunities. Our programs are designed to meet each community's unique needs, with the ultimate goal of building a more just, equitable society. We invite you to learn more about FISS and how you can get involved.
