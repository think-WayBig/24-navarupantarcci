Welcome to SGB FOUNDATION

Social work is a profession for those with a strong desire to help and improve people’s lives. It is not necessary to be a doctor, a nurse or a renowned person do service for others.

One just needs passion for the sufferings of others Swami Shankra Nand Ji Bhuri Wale is another name for such person who devoted his life to the noble but onerous mission of rescuing orphan, unwanted and unclaimed children and giving them a secure home and future. He aims to raise these children as healthy individuals and arm them with all the social skills and educational qualifications that they need to face life on their own terms.
